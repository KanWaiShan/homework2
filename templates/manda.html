The usage of Mandarin suffix-men
Linguistic literature notes that -Men have at least three main functions.
(1)-Men can only be used for human nouns. The non-human noun has an anthropomorphic effect.
(2)the Noun+ men must be definite
(3)-Men is not be allowed to co-occurring with the classification words in the NP, for instances (*Wǔ wèi xué shēng men ‘five students’)
