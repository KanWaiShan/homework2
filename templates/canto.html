The usage of Cantonese prefix -di and suffix dei
Linguistic literature notes that -Men have at least three main functions.
(1)di can be used for human nouns, inanimate nouns.
(2)di is not be allowed to co-occurring with the classification words in the NP, for instances (*五個啲學生 ‘five students’)
(3)dei can only be suffixed after proper nouns( we, they, you) In Cantonese, with a pronoun -dei is mandatory.
